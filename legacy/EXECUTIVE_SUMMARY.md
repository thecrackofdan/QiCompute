# Executive Summary

## Independent Reviewer Assessment

I would continue investing research effort into QiCompute, but I would pause major subsystem expansion.

The prototype is strong enough to test its core assumptions. It is not strong enough to justify production networking, public decentralization claims, real settlement, wallets, staking, governance, or smart contracts.

## Most Promising

- The principle is clean: Qi is mined, QiCompute moves Qi.
- Local escrow, refund, worker payable, treasury, receipt, and epoch accounting are concrete and well tested.
- Privacy-first local execution is a plausible wedge if real users care enough.
- Mining fallback gives a useful economic benchmark for worker behavior.
- Autonomous agents are a coherent fit because they can earn and spend in the same layer.
- The repo is honest about being a local-first research prototype.

## Most Uncertain

- Whether customers will pay for privacy, verification, or regional routing.
- Whether inference revenue can beat mining after real energy, hardware, failure, and verification costs.
- Whether useful-work verification can be strong without exposing private prompts or outputs.
- Whether reputation and committees survive adversarial public participation.
- Whether Qi circulates or is hoarded.
- Whether operators will tolerate the setup and maintenance burden.

## Direct Answer

QiCompute deserves continued validation, not broad new engineering.

The next dollar or hour should go into customer discovery, real GPU measurements, pilot operations, and adversarial verification tests. If those produce positive evidence, productionization becomes easier to justify. If they do not, more infrastructure will only make the project more complex without proving the market exists.

## Decision Standard

Continue only if the project can show:

- A real customer segment with willingness to pay.
- A measured mining/inference crossover on real hardware.
- A credible trust model for the first deployment context.
- Verification overhead that buyers and operators can accept.
- Evidence that Qi moves through useful work instead of sitting idle.
