# Research Findings

## Purpose

This document accumulates evidence for or against the QiCompute thesis over time. It should distinguish measured evidence from assumptions and simulations.

## Methodology

- Record benchmark results with hardware, runtime, model, duration, power estimate, token rate, request rate, utilization, energy estimate, and output hashes only.
- Record mining versus inference crossover measurements with explicit revenue, cost, fee, and verification assumptions.
- Record verification overhead as milliseconds per operation, throughput, and percentage overhead relative to inference duration.
- Record customer interviews as summarized findings, not raw sensitive customer data.
- Link evidence back to `ECONOMIC_ASSUMPTIONS.md` through the assumption tracker.

## Evidence Categories

- Hardware benchmarks.
- Mining/inference crossover.
- Verification overhead.
- Customer validation.
- Trusted operator pilots.
- Monetary circulation.
- Reputation and dispute behavior.
