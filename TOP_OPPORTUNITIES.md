# Top 10 Opportunities

| Rank | Opportunity | Why It Matters |
| --- | --- | --- |
| 1 | Privacy-first local compute | Strongest differentiation against centralized APIs if privacy claims become credible |
| 2 | Mining fallback as reservation price | Gives operators a rational baseline for when to serve inference |
| 3 | Autonomous agent economy | Agents can be customers, workers, verifiers, routers, and operators in one economic layer |
| 4 | Useful-work verification | Receipts, challenges, and committees could make compute marketplaces more auditable |
| 5 | Regional compute markets | Local power cost, privacy preference, and latency can create differentiated supply |
| 6 | Self-hoster/operator wedge | Early users may already own idle hardware and accept local-first tooling |
| 7 | No second issuance mechanism | Keeping Qi mining separate from marketplace rewards avoids a major incentive trap |
| 8 | Deterministic simulation suite | Assumptions can be tested and challenged before expensive infrastructure work |
| 9 | Escrow/refund accounting | Clear local settlement semantics make marketplace behavior easier to audit |
| 10 | Research credibility through honest limitations | Directly documenting gaps can prevent overbuilding and improve decision quality |

## Best Near-Term Opportunity

Validate a self-hoster or local-operator pilot where idle GPUs mine during low demand and serve private inference when paid jobs exceed the mining fallback floor.
